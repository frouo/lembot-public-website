# Lembot v2: Simpler, More Powerful

Today, we are excited to announce Lembot v2 üéâ

After listening to your feedback over the past years, we've completely redesigned the bot creation experience to make it simpler and more intuitive.

## What's new?

### One bot to rule them all

Remember when you had to choose between creating a "bot" or a "megabot"? That's gone.

In Lembot v2, there's just **one type of bot**. Simple, powerful, and flexible enough to handle everything from single campaigns to bulk exports.

### Very straightforward

Creating an export automation is now incredibly straightforward:

#### Step 1: Choose what to export

Select the data you want to export from your Lemlist campaigns:

- **Results** ‚Äî Overview of your campaign performance
- **Activities** ‚Äî Every email opened, clicked, replied, timestamped
- **Leads** ‚Äî All lead details with custom variables
- **Tasks** ‚Äî Task details, types, and due dates

![choose data type](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/395f4dcf-4532-4e4f-bd20-7bfcd91b62d5)

#### Step 2: Select your Lemlist team

Choose which Lemlist team to connect to (perfect if you manage multiple teams or clients).

#### Step 3: Choose the campaigns to export

This is where the magic happens. You now have **3 flexible options**:

**Manual selection**: Pick campaigns one by one from your list. Perfect when you want precise control.

![Manual selection](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bf0fdac9-60d2-41af-8413-1730f049f785)

**By tags**: Export all campaigns with specific tags automatically. Ideal for organizing exports by client, project, or campaign type.

![By tags selection](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/aefa5dcf-f670-4c19-84c6-4ac569fcac9d)

**All**: Export every active campaign at once. Set it and forget it - new campaigns are automatically included as long as they're active.

![All campaigns selection](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f0a8ba0f-1003-4f5e-83ac-86d7a2f3be0a)

## What happens to my old bots and megatbots?

Your existing bots and megabots will continue to work exactly as they do today.

However, they are now _deprecated_, which means we won't be adding new features or providing support for them going forward.

## Easy migration

We've made migration as painless as possible.

If you have custom columns in your old bots or megabots, simply:

1. Click "Copy fields" in your old bot
2. Paste them into your new v2 bot

That's it! Your custom configuration is preserved.
