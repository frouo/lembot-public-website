## FAQs

### Can I mix "By tags" and "All campaigns" in one Megabot?

Not in the same run ‚Äî choose one mode per Megabot. For combined exports, set up two Megabots: one for "By tags" and one for "All campaigns."

### What if I add new tags later?

In **By tags** mode, any new campaign tagged with your selected labels will automatically appear in your next export.

### Where do I find the exported sheet?

Your Google Sheet and worksheet are defined when you configure the Megabot ‚Äî open that Sheet in Google Drive to view the latest data.
